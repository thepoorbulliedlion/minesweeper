Description Minesweeper is a logic game, with the objective of clearing the map of the "mines"(orange squares). Left click to reveal a square, and right click to flag squares you believe to be mines. Revealed squares show how many mines are touching it in a one square radius, diagonals included. If the square has zero mines on it, the game conveniently auto-reveals all squares touching it.

DISCLAIMER

This game is not yet responsive, which means it's not meant to played on mobile. The first "reveal" of a square is also random as of now.
